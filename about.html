A Northern Uganda Music Icon

Born Maros Caesar Atine on 29 September 1985 in Bala, Kole, Uganda, Profesa Maros is a celebrated artist known for his vibrant afropop and dancehall music. He rose to prominence with his 2012 hit single Abonyo Otingere, earning a HiPipo nomination. With four albums, including White Sand (2024) and Citadel, he has won 3 HiPipo Music Awards, 9 Lango Entertainment Awards, and the 2021 Janzi Award for Cente Pe. As President of the Lango Entertainers Union and a board member of WALK Creative Arts Foundation, Profesa Maros is a cultural leader, blending music, comedy, and community impact.
